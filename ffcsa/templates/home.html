Raising Animals Simply, to Simply Raise the Bar

We aspire to create a regenerative model that improves the planet and does not deplete resources like other industrial mono cropped agriculture systems.

We are a resource to the aspiring generations of young farmers.

We make no compromises in practicing what we believe to be the necessary future of farming.

We actively engage in conservation initiatives on the land.

We support our local food community.

We take a long-term approach with our sustainable livestock operation.

We pursue ongoing improvement to the health of the land on and around our farm.

We do not use herbicides, pesticides, growth hormones, sub-therapeutic antibiotics or synthetic nitrogen fertilizers.

We generate soil fertility through the natural cycles of composting, grazing management, and seeding a variety of forages.

We are 100% transparent with what our animals eat.

We regenerate the soil with composted manure and minerals which replenishes the nutrients needed to grow lush forage.
